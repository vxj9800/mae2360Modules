Data Types

  • int - The range of integer actually depends on the hardware and compiler. The -2,147,483,648 to 2,147,483,647 range is standard.
  • float - These take care of most non-scientific computations.
  • double - An extension of float, these are more appropriate for engineering and scientific applications when additional precision is needed.
  • char - represents a single ASCII character. These must be enclosed by a single quotation mark (').
